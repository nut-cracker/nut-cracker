ABOUT

The Career Development Centre of IISER Bhopal is established to provide an interface between the students and companies for mutual benefit. The CDC is committed not only for job hunting but also for the overall guidance of a student in Career exploration, job search strategies and in-campus interviews for recruitment. The CDC shall be guiding the students in their choice of academic courses, from the wide array available, so as to maximize a student's chance to land a rewarding and exciting career opportunity.

ABOUT

1. Organized an Open Interactive Session on April 14, 2013 with all undergraduate students describing career opportunities in each major discipline.


2. Upon the request of the CDC, Mr. Ryan Pereira from the US-India Educational Foundation visited IISER Bhopal on September 6, 2013, to deliver a talk titled Applying for Doctoral studies to the US.


3. The CDC, together with the Office of Student's Affairs, plans to start an Alumni Association to provide a platform for former students to contribute to the growth of IISER Bhopal.

INTERNSHIPS

One of the most important phase of a student's life in IISER Bhopal is the internship that he/she does in the summer vacation. Internship provides the exposure which a student needs for knowing the difference between learning inside a classroom and learning with hands-on experience. The CDC shall be guiding students for the internship opportunities as well.

ALUMNI

IISER BHOPAL

Being the premier science research institutes in India, IISER's are the new destination for students interested in science. The students here are screened effectively so that wherever you see, you shall find talented and hardworking undergrads. These undergrads are shaped under the guidance of some of the best teachers available. Along with strong fundamental concepts, the students also have the experience in computer coding and hands-on experience in using various up-to-date instruments that facilitate their research. The interdisciplinary programme and research intensive training bring out the best in students so that you gain an invaluable addition to your organisation. Please send an email to placementcell@iiserb.ac.in and give us information about your company and the kind of work you do.
